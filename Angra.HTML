WHO IS ANGRA?

Angra is a Brazilian metal band formed in 1991 that has gone through
a number of line-up changes since its foundation.
They have released eight regular studio albums, five EPs and three
live CD/DVD’s to date. Led by Rafael Bittencourt,
the band has gained a degree of popularity in Japan and Europe.
