Quem é Venom?

Venom are an English heavy metal band
formed in Newcastle upon Tyne in 1978.
Coming to prominence towards the end of the
new wave of British heavy metal, Venom's first two albums:
Welcome to Hell (1981) and Black Metal (1982),
are considered major influences on thrash metal and extreme metal in general.
